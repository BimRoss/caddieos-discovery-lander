Timeline & scope

How long is the engagement?
60 calendar days from signature. If Dale materially delays delivery of required access — CaddieOS documentation, walkthrough, code repos, sandbox, dev-team Q&A, or the warm-intro pipeline — the 60-day timeline shifts day-for-day.
What if you need to expand scope?
Material scope changes require a written addendum with adjusted fee and timeline. We don't silently absorb new asks — we flag, scope, and price them. That's a feature, not friction.
When does the timer start?
Day 1 is the signature date. Personal agents (mine, Steve's, Dave's) plus the shared CaddieOS team agent are provisioned that same day. No ramp lag.
What's the final deliverable?
A written Discovery Findings & Recommendations report at day 60, plus a live 60–90 minute working session with Dale leadership to walk through it. By mutual agreement, the session may extend by 30 minutes during the meeting itself.
What's in the report?
Module keep / cut / expand calls (each grounded in research). Named beachhead segment. Pricing hypothesis tested against three live A/B/C models with real operators. Build-vs-partner-vs-buy decisions for every adjacent capability. A 12-month roadmap ranked by ARR-per-dollar-built.

Fee & payment

What's the total fee?
$102,000 USD, fixed fee. Split evenly between the two primary consultants ($51,000 each). Out-of-pocket expenses absorbed up to a $2,500 aggregate cap; above the cap, reimbursed by Dale at cost with prior written approval.
How does payment work?
50% non-refundable on signature, 50% non-refundable on delivery of the final report. Each primary paid separately — $25,500 each at signing, $25,500 each at delivery. ACH preferred, wire or check accepted. Net 7 from invoice. Late payments accrue 1.5%/month or max allowed by law.
What's the acceptance trigger?
Deliverable is deemed accepted if Dale doesn't object in writing within 7 calendar days of delivery with specific deficiencies named. Final payment is due net 7 from acceptance or deemed acceptance.
What if Dale terminates early?
Either party may terminate for material breach with 20 days' written notice and a chance to cure. Dale owes for work completed through termination date. The first 50% deposit remains non-refundable in all cases. Termination for convenience: 100% of the fee remains owed.
Out-of-pocket expenses?
We absorb up to $2,500 aggregate across both primary consultants (travel, per diem, interview incentives, paid third-party research). If projected expenses are about to exceed the cap, we pause and ask. Above the cap is reimbursed by Dale at cost with prior written approval.
