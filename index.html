Frequently asked · pulled from the proposal

Anything we missed? Probably here.

Timeline & deliverable

How long is the engagement?
60 calendar days from signature. Material access delays (CaddieOS walkthrough, code repos, dev-team Q&A, warm-intro pipeline) shift the timeline day-for-day.
What's the final deliverable?
A written Discovery Findings & Recommendations report at day 60, plus a live 60–90 minute working session with Dale leadership to walk through it.
What's in the report?
Module keep/cut/expand calls. Named beachhead segment. Pricing hypothesis tested against three live A/B/C models. Build-vs-partner-vs-buy on every adjacent capability. 12-month roadmap ranked by ARR-per-dollar-built.
Scope expansion?
Material changes require a written addendum with adjusted fee and timeline. We flag, scope, and price — not silently absorb.

Fee & payment

Total fee?
$102,000 USD, fixed fee, split 50/50 between John and Steve. Out-of-pocket expenses absorbed up to a $2,500 aggregate cap.
Payment structure?
50% non-refundable on signature, 50% non-refundable on delivery. $25,500 each consultant at each milestone. ACH preferred. Net 7 from invoice.
Acceptance trigger?
Deemed accepted if Dale doesn't object in writing with specific deficiencies within 7 calendar days of delivery.
Termination?
Either party may terminate for material breach with 20 days' notice and cure window. Dale owes for work completed through termination date. Initial 50% deposit non-refundable in all cases.

Team & AI

Who is Dave Bisbee?
Senior support consultant. Founder of Back on Course Golf™. 30+ years across some of the country's most respected private clubs. 50,000+ golfers coached. John and Steve direct the engagement and draw on Dave's network at their discretion.
How is Dave compensated?
Allocated from the engagement fee by the primary consultants, at their discretion. Not separately contracted by Dale. Surfaced on the bi-weekly status cadence.

The 4th consultant. Personal CaddieOS-tuned agents for John, Steve, Dave plus a shared team agent holding full project context. In scope. No add-on cost.
AI confidentiality?
Industry-standard practices. Encrypted at rest, scoped access, secure transmission. Dale's data is not used to train any model. Runs on our infrastructure, not a public chatbot.

Consultants may engage other clients, provided they're not directly competitive with CaddieOS. Restriction ends on final delivery. Steve has disclosed a non-competitive interest in a smart-ball / mobile-simulator venture.
Post-engagement support?
